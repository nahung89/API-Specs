
***** SPECIAL PAGE *****

- get special info & tracks & original videos of each track
	-> GET /v4.1/special/{special_id}
	[!] Track’s structure: need `id`, `uri`, `duration`
	[!] Video’s structure (3 item max) of each track: need original video info + seek time to track

- collect special
	-> POST /v4.1/users/me/collectedSpecial
	-> Form/JSON param: id=xxx

- discard special
	[!?] DELETE /v4.1/users/me/collectedSpecial
	[!?] Form/JSON param: id=xxx
